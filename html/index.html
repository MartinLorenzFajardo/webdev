#DataBreachAlert: A MAJOR DATA BREACH HITS TGE SUPREME COURT OF THE PHILIPPINES- SENSITIVE LEGAL INFORMATION EXPOSED

Date Published: August 28, 2024

On August 27, 2024, the Supreme Court of the Philippines experienced a major data breach, leading to the exposure of sensitive legal information. The breach, disclosed by a threat actor named "grep," included 13,564 rows of data, affecting various legal applications, court cases, and other services. What Was Exposed? - Assessment Numbers - Full Names - Case Categories and Types - Payment Statuses - Dates Filed and Paid. The breach has raised serious concerns about the protection of confidential information within the country's judicial system. Immediate action is expected to be taken to address the breach and safeguard against future incidents.
